Here's our Jan 6, 2026 release!
This release has is mainly a cleanup and bug-fixing release, with some updated figures for the transformer in various chapters.
The August release made larger changes, including DPO in chapter 9, new ASR and TTS chapters, a restructured LLM chapter, and
unicode in Chapter 2.
Individual chapters and updated slides are below.
Here is a single pdf of Jan 6, 2026 book!
Feel free to use the draft chapters and slides in your classes
, print it out, whatever, the resulting feedback we get from you makes the book better!
Typos and comments
are very welcome (just email

and let us know the date on the draft)!
