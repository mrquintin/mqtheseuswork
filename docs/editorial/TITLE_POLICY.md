# Public Title Policy

Public article and conclusion titles should be written in normal title or sentence case and should stay at or below 70 characters.

The 70-character ceiling is advisory in the reader path: titles longer than 70 characters log a warning with the `[title-policy]` prefix, but publication is not blocked. Generator-side enforcement is handled separately so existing published records remain readable.

Titles should not rely on CSS uppercasing, inscription fonts, or wide letter spacing to create hierarchy. The public reader preserves the stored title text and handles display through the EB Garamond title stack.
