I think the most important lesson from this quarter is that we kept underestimating the cost of acquisition.

My own view is that we should have rebalanced toward retention by month two; our hand was forced by the churn cohort that we onboarded in January.

I am still convinced this team is the right team — we just need to stop framing the problem as a top-of-funnel problem when our data clearly says it is a retention problem.
