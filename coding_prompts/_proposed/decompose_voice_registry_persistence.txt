"resolves OR registers a founder". In practice
`noosphere/noosphere/methods/decompose_voice.py`:

  `FounderRegistry` and were not carried into the registered method.

Why it matters: a TRANSFORMATION that claims to register founders but
does not is a silent no-op for every new speaker, and the
non-determinism (fresh UUID per call) breaks attribution stability —
the exact thing the method exists to provide.
