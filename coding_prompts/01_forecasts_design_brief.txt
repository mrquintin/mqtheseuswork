### 3. Data model — full table list
For every new table: name, columns with types, indexes, and FK relationships. Tables (minimum):
- `ForecastMarket` — external market mirror (id, source enum POLYMARKET|KALSHI, externalId, title, description, resolutionCriteria, category, currentYesPrice, currentNoPrice, volume, openTime, closeTime, resolvedAt, resolvedOutcome, raw payload JSON, statusEnum)
- `ForecastPrediction` — model's principled prediction for a market (id, marketId FK, organizationId, probabilityYes, confidenceLow, confidenceHigh, headline, reasoning, status enum PUBLISHED|ABSTAINED_*, abstentionReason, modelName, promptTokens, completionTokens, createdAt)
- `ForecastCitation` — verbatim citation, mirrors `OpinionCitation` (predictionId FK, sourceType, sourceId, quotedSpan, supportLabel)
- `ForecastResolution` — terminal record (predictionId FK, marketOutcome, brierScore, logLoss, calibrationBucket, resolvedAt, justification text — auto-filled from market resolution metadata)
- `ForecastBet` — single record covering both paper and live modes (predictionId FK, mode enum PAPER|LIVE, exchange enum POLYMARKET|KALSHI, side enum YES|NO, stakeUsd, entryPrice, exitPrice, status enum PENDING|FILLED|CANCELLED|SETTLED, externalOrderId, settlement P&L, createdAt, settledAt). Live rows always have `live_authorized_at` set.
- `ForecastPortfolioState` — singleton-per-org (paperBalance, liveBalanceUsd, dailyLossUsd, dailyLossResetAt, killSwitchEngaged, killSwitchReason, updatedAt)
- `ForecastFollowUpSession` + `ForecastFollowUpMessage` — same shape as Currents follow-up tables, scoped to a prediction.
