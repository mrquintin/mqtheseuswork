## The problem

The coherence review queue at `/q/review` shows each review item with:
```
pair abc12345… / def67890…
```

These are truncated UUIDs of `claimAId` and `claimBId`. The human reviewer has no way to see what the actual claims say without leaving the page and manually looking them up. The `reason` field provides narrative context, but the claims themselves — the two statements allegedly in tension — are invisible. This makes the review queue nearly useless for its intended purpose of human-in-the-loop judgment.

## Your task

Display the actual text of both claims (claim A and claim B) in each review queue item, so the human reviewer can see the two statements and judge whether they cohere, contradict, or are unresolved.
