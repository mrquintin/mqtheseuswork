hookup. Important reality check: Robinhood does not publish a
supported retail trading API, and unofficial libraries are
periodically broken by Robinhood's app updates. Alpaca, in
contrast, ships an official, well-maintained API with a paper
trading environment that is the industry standard for what the
founder described. Plan:

 - Robinhood remains as a documented "if you really want it"
   adapter behind an explicit flag (prompt 62 implements it
   thinly, with the caveat).

Implement:

A. New entities. Add to Prisma and SQLModel:
   - `EquityInstrument`: a tradeable stock or ETF.
     Columns: `symbol`, `exchange`, `assetClass` (`STOCK | ETF |
     ADR`), `name`, `cusip` (nullable), `figi` (nullable),
     `isTradable`, `lastPrice`, `lastPriceAt`, `currency`,
     `createdAt`, `updatedAt`. Compound unique key on
     `(symbol, exchange)`.
   - `EquityPriceTick`: append-only price history.
     Columns: `instrumentId` FK, `ts`, `open`, `high`, `low`,
     `close`, `volume`, `source` (enum
     `ALPACA | ROBINHOOD | YFINANCE | MANUAL`). Indexed on
     `(instrumentId, ts DESC)`.
   - `EquitySignal`: a model-generated bullish / bearish /
     neutral take on an instrument, grounded in citations
     from the Noosphere knowledge base (same verbatim-anchor
     rule as ForecastCitation).
     Columns: `instrumentId` FK, `direction` (enum
     `BULLISH | BEARISH | NEUTRAL | ABSTAINED`),
     `confidenceLow`, `confidenceHigh`, `targetPriceLow`
     (nullable), `targetPriceHigh` (nullable), `horizonDays`,
     `headline`, `reasoning`, `modelName`, `promptTokens`,
     `completionTokens`, `status` (enum
     `PUBLISHED | ABSTAINED | REVOKED`), `abstentionReason`,
     `createdAt`, `updatedAt`.
   - `EquitySignalCitation`: verbatim-anchored citation to a
     Conclusion or Claim or Principle (post-prompt-56).
     Columns: `signalId` FK, `sourceType`, `sourceId`,
     `quotedSpan`, `supportLabel`.
   - `EquityPosition`: a paper or live position derived from
     a signal. Columns: `signalId` FK, `instrumentId` FK,
     `mode` (`PAPER | LIVE`), `side` (`LONG | SHORT |
     CASH_RESERVE`), `qty`, `entryPrice`, `entryAt`,
     `exitPrice`, `exitAt`, `status` (`PENDING | OPEN |
     CLOSED | CANCELLED | FAILED`),
     `externalOrderId` (nullable; live only),
     `realizedPnlUsd` (nullable; closed only),
     `unrealizedPnlUsd` (computed; nullable),
     `liveAuthorizedAt` (nullable, mirrors ForecastBet's
     gate-3 field), `createdAt`, `updatedAt`.
   - `EquityPortfolioState`: singleton per org. Columns:
     `organizationId`, `paperBalanceUsd`, `liveBalanceUsd`,
     `dailyLossUsd`, `dailyLossWindowResetAt`,
     `killSwitchEngaged Boolean @default(false)`,
     `killSwitchReason`, `updatedAt`. Unique on
     `organizationId`.
