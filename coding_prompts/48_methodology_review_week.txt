Goal: schedule and tool the firm's first "Methodology Review Week" —
a structured, time-boxed period where the firm focuses exclusively
on reviewing its own methods. This is the firm putting its
methodological commitments into practice as a calendared event,
not as a vague intention.

Before you start — read:
1. The methods registry, MQS spec, drift detector, retirement
   workflow (Round 17–18).
2. The seasonal review (prompt 46) — methodology review week
   produces inputs for it.

Implement:

A. Schedule. Set up a recurring quarterly Methodology Review
   Week (5 working days). The schedule lives in the firm's
   own scheduler, with named day-by-day focus:
   Day 1: drift events review
   Day 2: failure-mode catalog review
   Day 3: domain-bound review
   Day 4: retirement candidate review
   Day 5: write the methodology section of the seasonal
   review

B. Tooling. Each day surfaces a focused queue at
   `(authed)/methodology-review-week/<day>/page.tsx`. The
   queue is a curated subset of the unified attention queue
   (Round 17 prompt 34) filtered to the day's focus.

C. Day-end checkpoints. Each day's tooling expects a written
   summary by end-of-day. The summary feeds the seasonal
   review's methodology section.

D. Review-week historical archive. Past review weeks are
   browseable at
   `(authed)/methodology-review-week/history/page.tsx`. Past
   summaries are signed and immutable.

E. Public hint. The public methodology page surfaces "Last
   review week: <date>; next review week: <date>" so outside
   readers know the firm is on a cadence.

F. Tests. Schedule generation; queue filtering per day;
   summary persistence; signed-summary round trip.

Constraints
- Review Week is opt-in per founder. The schedule does not
  punish missed weeks; it logs them and continues.
- Summaries are not auto-generated; the founder writes them.
  The agent can DRAFT a summary based on the day's queue,
  but the draft is clearly a draft and the founder's edits
  are tracked.
- Public-facing dates are accurate. If a review week is
  postponed, the public hint updates.
