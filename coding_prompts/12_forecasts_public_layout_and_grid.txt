### 3. ForecastCard component

`theseus-codex/src/app/forecasts/ForecastCard.tsx`. Visual contract:

- Headline (the model's, NOT the market title ‚ÄĒ the headline is the thesis).
- Probability bar: 0‚Äď100% YES with text overlay `64% YES`.
- Below the bar: `Market: 0.58` (the implied market price); a small chevron (`+0.06`) showing the model's edge if any. Color the chevron green for positive, red for negative; muted if < 0.05.
- Status pill: PUBLISHED | RESOLVED-CORRECT | RESOLVED-INCORRECT | RESOLVED-CANCELLED. Use the `--forecasts-prob-*` palette.
- Metadata row: `Polymarket` or `Kalshi` source badge, market category, time-to-resolution (e.g., "resolves in 12d").
- Citation count: `‚Ė¶ 4 sources`.
- Tap target leads to `/forecasts/[id]`.
