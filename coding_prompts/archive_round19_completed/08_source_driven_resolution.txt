Goal: invert the contradiction resolution model per the founder's
explicit instruction in the meeting: "I actually kind of want to
remove that because they should be resolved only by the sources
themselves again." Remove the manual contradiction-resolution UI.
Contradictions stand until new sources arrive that either
strengthen one side, weaken the other, or introduce a synthesis
principle that supersedes both.

This is an epistemological change, not just a UI change. The
firm's database becomes a faithful crystallisation of what the
sources jointly imply, not a curated narrative.

What the new model REQUIRES:

1. Contradictions persist as first-class entities (already added
   in prompt 06). They are never "resolved" by an operator
   clicking a button. They are either:
   - **Resolved by source ingestion** — a new source supports one
     side, weakens the other, OR introduces a synthesis principle
     that subsumes both. Resolution is automatic and grounded.
   - **Acknowledged as standing** — the founder confirms the
     contradiction is genuine and the firm should hold both
     positions until evidence accumulates. This is a recorded
     status, not a resolution.
   - **Disputed as detection error** — the founder believes the
     engine got it wrong; the contradiction is downgraded and a
     dispute logged for engine calibration.

2. A new "contradiction lifecycle" replaces the manual-resolve
   pattern:

   ```
   DETECTED → STANDING ⇄ WEAKENED ⇄ RESOLVED-BY-SOURCE
                     ↘ DISPUTED-AS-ERROR (terminal)
                     ↘ SUBSUMED-BY-SYNTHESIS (terminal)
   ```

3. Auto-resolution triggers (when source ingestion fires the
   transition):

   - **WEAKENED**: a new principle is added whose embedding sits
     between the two contradictory principles AND whose
     contradiction score with one side is significantly lower
     than with the other. The lower-scoring side becomes the
     supported side.
   - **RESOLVED-BY-SOURCE**: a new principle is added whose
     contradiction score is < 0.30 with one side AND > 0.65 with
     the other. The contradiction is marked resolved in favor of
     the low-scoring side. The "weight of evidence" tally is
     persisted on the ContradictionLifecycle row.
   - **SUBSUMED-BY-SYNTHESIS**: a new principle is added that the
     synthesis engine (prompt 10) flags as a candidate synthesis
     of the contradictory pair. The founder confirms via a
     dedicated triage card; on accept, both original principles
     are flagged "subsumed by <new id>" and the contradiction is
     marked SUBSUMED.
