# Codex Prompt 4 of 8 — Year-end podcast statistics

**Round:** Podcast conversation geometry. **Sequence:** 4 of 8.
**Report back with:** the season/year visibility rule and the aggregate fields rendered.

## Intent

The voice memo says a weighting statistic should probably be visible only at the end of a podcast season or year, because showing it while conversations are ongoing could steer how people talk. Implement that principle directly.

## Before You Start

1. Read `buildYearEndConversationStats` in `theseus-codex/src/lib/conversationGeometry.ts`.
2. Read the year-end card in `ConversationGeometryPanel.tsx`.
3. Confirm transcript page queries are scoped to the signed-in founder's organization and visibility rules.

## Task

For the current transcript's calendar year, query same-organization transcript/audio uploads and build an aggregate:

- transcript count.
- total turns.
- total handoffs.
- participant count.
- center-of-gravity speaker by word share.
- strongest speaker bridge.

If the year is still open, render a locked card and do not reveal those aggregate values. If the year is closed, reveal the aggregate.

## Verification

- Unit tests cover locked and ready states.
- The transcript page passes type-check with the new query.
- The query respects organization, deletion, and private-upload visibility.

## Definition of Done

- Current-year aggregate values are not shown before year close.
- Past-year aggregate values can render without a new migration.
- The card is stable when there are zero or one transcripts.
