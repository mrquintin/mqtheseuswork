### Scoring math

For every resolved (non-cancelled) prediction:

- **Brier score:** `(probability_yes - actual)^2` where `actual` is `1.0` for YES outcome and `0.0` for NO. Lower is better. Range 0–1.
- **Log loss:** `-actual * ln(p) - (1-actual) * ln(1-p)` with `p = clamp(probability_yes, 1e-6, 1 - 1e-6)`.
- **Calibration bucket:** floor(probability_yes * 10) / 10. So 0.73 → 0.7. Each ForecastResolution stores the bucket; aggregate calibration is computed by selecting all resolutions, grouping by bucket, and computing the empirical YES-rate per bucket. The aggregate is computed on read (in the API), not stored.

### Aggregate update on `ForecastPortfolioState`

After each resolution write:
- bump `totalResolved`
- recompute and persist `meanBrier` and `meanLogLoss` over the last 90 days
- never delete; this is append-only history
