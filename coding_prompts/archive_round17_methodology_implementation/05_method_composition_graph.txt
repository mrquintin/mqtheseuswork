`extract_claims`, `nli_scorer`, `six_layer_coherence`, and so on. When a
downstream method drifts (prompt 04) or has a high-severity unresolved
failure mode (prompt 03), every method that composes it should inherit
risk. Without the DAG, that risk inheritance is invisible.
