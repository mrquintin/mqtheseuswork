Goal: an ablation study on the Householder reflection step inside the
contradiction-geometry pipeline. The reflection is part of the firm's
unique claim — that contradicting claims live in a learned reflection
direction in embedding space. The ablation answers: what does the
reflection contribute, separately from the difference vector and from
the learned direction? Is it pulling its weight or is it an artifact
inherited from an earlier prototype?

A. Variant runners in
   `noosphere/noosphere/benchmarks/qh_ablations.py`:

       unit vector, fixed seed)
